Why Server-Side Blocking?

Traditional web filters like Lightspeed, Securly, and GoGuardian constantly struggle to keep up with hundreds of proxy URLs. Our solution eliminates that burden entirely.

Guaranteed Effectiveness

Block access at the server level—no URL lists to update, no cat-and-mouse games with new domains. When blocked, it stays blocked.

Rapid Deployment

Implementation takes anywhere from 1 hour to 24 hours. Once activated, all district IP ranges are immediately protected.

Reduce IT Workload

Your network security team no longer needs to manually block hundreds of URLs. We handle it server-side, permanently.

Choose Your Protection Level

Select the plan that best fits your district's needs. Both options provide complete server-side blocking with no additional configuration required.

Public IP Address Required

During checkout, you will be asked to provide your district's public IP address. This is critical. The public IP must be the shared external IP address that all district users connect through. All devices on your network should share this same public IP. Without the correct IP address, server-side blocking cannot be applied to your district.

Service Terms

Void Network reserves the right to discontinue server-side blocking services at any time and for any reason. If we choose to end this service, your subscription will be cancelled and access will be restored to your district. While we are committed to providing reliable protection, this is not a guaranteed permanent solution—however, as long as your subscription remains active, blocking will remain in effect.
